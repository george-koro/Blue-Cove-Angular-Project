Tour in athens

Discover the splendor of Classical Athens and experience the vibrant heart of the city on this full day excursion. Your guide will meet you in the lobby of your hotel and you will make your way the iconic expression of Greek culture: the Acropolis.

Walk up the same steps that the ancient trod and view the temples where they worshiped the goddess Athena the protectress of the city, Poseidon god of the seas and Zeus, king of the gods.

Here You’ll discover some of the world’s greatest architectural achievements, including the Parthenon. Afterwards, walk down to the Acropolis Museum and view the stunning artwork and artifacts that adorned the temples and their surroundings.

Afterwards, you will proceed way through the historical center to a restaurant where you will enjoy a mouthwatering traditional lunch. (Note: lunch is not included and will be a la carte).
